       - State:
         - `private val _transcript = MutableStateFlow<List<ChatMessage>>(emptyList())`
         - `val transcript: StateFlow<List<ChatMessage>>`

         - `JsonMessage(type="response")` → parse payload JSON for "text" field, append `ChatMessage(id=UUID, text=text, isUser=false)` to transcript

       - `fun clearTranscript()` → reset transcript to empty list (called on session end after feedback)
